If your campaign process feels inconsistent, a checklist solves it. This one keeps your Google Sheets email workflow clean and repeatable.

## Before sending

- Validate contact quality.
- Confirm required columns are filled.
- Review variables in subject and body.
- Segment recipients by goal.

## Sending phase

- Preview sample emails.
- Send in clear batches.
- Monitor first responses quickly.

## After sending

- Update status for each contact.
- Mark who replied.
- Schedule follow-ups.
- Note what worked.

## Minimal columns to keep

```
Email | FirstName | Company | Segment | Status | NextStep
```

## Conclusion

A checklist reduces errors and improves consistency. Good campaigns are usually the result of simple habits done every time.
