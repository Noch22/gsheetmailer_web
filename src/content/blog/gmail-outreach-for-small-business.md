Small businesses need efficient outreach, but many tools are expensive or overbuilt. A practical alternative is Google Sheets + Gmail with personalized templates.

## Why this setup works for small teams

- Low cost and easy to adopt.
- Minimal training required.
- Messages are sent from your real Gmail identity.
- Fast iteration when you test new angles.

## Recommended data fields

| Email | FirstName | Company | Industry | Offer |
|---|---|---|---|---|

These five fields are enough to create relevant outreach messages.

## Outreach template example

```
Subject: Idea for {{Company}}

Hi {{FirstName}},

I work with teams in {{Industry}} and noticed
an opportunity around {{Offer}}.

Happy to share a quick example if useful.
```

## Quick performance metrics

- Open rate
- Reply rate
- Positive reply rate
- Meetings booked

Track those in your sheet each week.

## Conclusion

You do not need enterprise software to run solid outreach. A simple workflow with good targeting can produce reliable results.
