Nonprofits need simple communication tools that are affordable and easy to manage. Google Sheets and Gmail offer a strong baseline workflow.

## Typical nonprofit campaigns

- Event invitations
- Volunteer updates
- Donor thank-you messages
- Donation reminders

## Suggested contact sheet

| Email | FirstName | City | Segment | LastDonation |
|---|---|---|---|---|

This gives enough context to personalize each message.

## Example donor email

```
Subject: Thank you, {{FirstName}}

Hi {{FirstName}},

Thank you for supporting our work in {{City}}.
Your contribution makes a real local impact.
```

## Conclusion

You can run meaningful nonprofit email campaigns without complex software. Clarity, segmentation, and consistency are what matter most.
