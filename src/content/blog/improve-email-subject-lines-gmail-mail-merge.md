Subject lines decide whether your email gets opened. Even small changes can make a measurable difference.

## What good subject lines have in common

- Clear context
- Natural wording
- Short length
- Relevant personalization

## Ready-to-use subject formulas

- "{{FirstName}}, quick question about {{Topic}}"
- "Idea for {{Company}}"
- "Following up on {{Topic}}"
- "Can I share a short example?"

## Common mistakes

- Too many symbols or all caps
- Overly promotional language
- Subject lines that are too long on mobile
- No relevance to recipient context

## Quick A/B process

Split your list into two equal groups and test two subject lines. Keep the winner for your next batches.

## Conclusion

Better subject lines are one of the fastest ways to improve campaign performance. Test often and keep what works.
