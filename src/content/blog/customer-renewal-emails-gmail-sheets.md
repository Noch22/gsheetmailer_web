Renewals are often won or lost on follow-up quality. A short, timely reminder can prevent churn and improve retention.

## Suggested schedule

- 30 days before renewal.
- 7 days before renewal.
- 1 day before renewal.

## Recommended columns

| Email | FirstName | Plan | RenewalDate | Status |
|---|---|---|---|---|

This is enough to personalize and track every reminder.

## Renewal email example

```
Subject: Your {{Plan}} plan renews on {{RenewalDate}}

Hi {{FirstName}},

Quick reminder that your {{Plan}} plan renews on {{RenewalDate}}.
If you want to review options before renewal, I can help.
```

## Conclusion

Renewal emails do not need to be complicated. A simple, personalized sequence keeps customers informed and reduces avoidable churn.
