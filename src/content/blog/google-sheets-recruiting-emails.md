Recruiting teams often lose time rewriting similar messages. A structured sheet plus personalized templates solves that problem quickly.

## Useful columns for recruiting

```
Email | FirstName | Role | CurrentCompany | Stage | NextAction
```

Use `Stage` to switch between first contact, follow-up, and interview invite templates.

## First outreach example

```
Subject: {{Role}} opportunity - quick chat?

Hi {{FirstName}},

Your background in {{Role}} caught our attention.
Would you be open to a quick conversation this week?
```

## Why this is effective

- Faster outreach with consistent quality.
- Personal tone from your real Gmail account.
- Clear tracking in one place.

## Conclusion

Recruiting outreach does not need a complex tool stack. A simple workflow can improve speed and consistency immediately.
