Personalization is the difference between ignored outreach and real conversations. The goal is not to write a novel, but to prove relevance quickly.

## Keep personalization lightweight

Use 2 or 3 variables only:

- First name
- Company
- One relevant context point

Too much personalization slows execution without clear gains.

## Sheet structure

| Email | FirstName | Company | Context | Status |
|---|---|---|---|---|

The `Context` column is where your relevance comes from.

## Cold email example

```
Subject: {{FirstName}}, quick idea for {{Company}}

Hi {{FirstName}},

I noticed {{Context}} at {{Company}} and thought
this short idea might be relevant.

Open to a quick exchange?
```

## Conclusion

Effective cold email personalization is simple: relevant context, short copy, and consistent follow-up. Google Sheets makes that process easy to maintain.
