Webinar attendance starts with strong invitation emails. Generic blasts underperform, while targeted invitations convert better.

## Segment before you send

- Existing customers
- Warm leads
- Partners
- Community subscribers

Each segment should get a slightly different message.

## Template example

```
Subject: {{FirstName}}, save your seat for our webinar

Hi {{FirstName}},

We're hosting a webinar on {{Topic}} on {{Date}}.
Given your role at {{Company}}, this may be relevant.

Register here: {{RegistrationLink}}
```

## Reminder sequence

1. Initial invite.
2. Reminder after 3 days.
3. Final reminder 24h before event.

## Best practices

- Keep the value proposition clear.
- Use short emails.
- Add one clear call to action.
- Track registered vs not registered in your sheet.

## Conclusion

Personalized webinar invitations are easy to run with a lightweight workflow. Better segmentation usually means better attendance.
