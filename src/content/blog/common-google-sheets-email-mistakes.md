Google Sheets email campaigns are simple, but small mistakes can hurt results fast. Here are the most common ones.

## 7 frequent mistakes

1. Poor list quality.
2. Generic subject lines.
3. Overly long first emails.
4. Broken personalization variables.
5. No preview before sending.
6. Follow-ups sent too soon.
7. No response tracking.

## Fast fixes

- Clean your list before each campaign.
- Preview at least 3 sample rows.
- Keep emails concise and specific.
- Add a `Status` column and update it daily.

## Pre-send checklist

- Variables resolved
- Subject line validated
- Segment selected
- Final proofread complete

## Conclusion

Most campaign issues are process issues, not tool issues. A clean workflow fixes most of them quickly.
