A product launch email works best when people feel it is relevant to them. You do not need a heavy marketing stack to do this well.

## Step 1: segment your contact list

Create segments like active customers, inactive customers, and warm prospects. Keep segment data in your sheet.

## Step 2: write one angle per segment

Subject: A new update you may care about, {{FirstName}}

Hi {{FirstName}},

We just launched [feature or offer]. Based on your use case, the main benefit is [specific value].

Would you like a quick walkthrough?

## Step 3: send in waves and adapt

Start with a small wave, review replies, and adjust subject lines or opening copy before the next wave.

## Checklist

- Segment before writing
- Use one clear benefit per message
- Keep CTA simple
- Track replies in the sheet
- Plan a short follow-up

## Mistakes to avoid

- Same message for all segments
- Too many claims in one email
- No clear next step
- Ignoring reply patterns

## FAQ

### How many segments should I start with?
Two to four is enough for most launch campaigns.

### Should I list every feature?
No, lead with the most relevant outcome.

### Can I follow up after a few days?
Yes, with a different angle and concise CTA.
