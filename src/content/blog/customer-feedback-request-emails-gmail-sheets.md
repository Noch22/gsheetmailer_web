Customer feedback helps prioritize product improvements. You can run a clean feedback campaign with Gmail and Google Sheets in a few steps.

## Step 1: select the right contacts

Target active or recently served customers. Track `Email`, `FirstName`, `PlanOrUseCase`, `LastInteraction`, and `FeedbackStatus`.

## Step 2: send a focused request

Subject: Quick feedback request, {{FirstName}}

Hi {{FirstName}},

Thanks for using our service. Could you share one thing that works well and one thing we should improve?

A short reply is perfect.

## Step 3: categorize replies for action

After sending, classify feedback by theme in your sheet (onboarding, usability, support, outcomes) and assign next actions.

## Checklist

- Target relevant customer segments
- Ask one or two clear questions
- Keep response path easy
- Categorize feedback themes
- Turn insights into concrete tasks

## Mistakes to avoid

- Long surveys for busy users
- Vague questions
- No follow-through on insights
- Contacting the same users too often

## FAQ

### What response rate should I expect?
Even a moderate rate is useful if answers are actionable.

### Do I need incentives?
Not always. Clear and short requests often work.

### How often should I run this campaign?
Quarterly is a practical cadence for many teams.

## Conclusion

A strong feedback loop depends on clarity and execution. Install GSheetMailer to run personalized feedback requests directly from Gmail.
